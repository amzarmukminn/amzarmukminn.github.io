Personal Philosophy

Knowledge without character is dangerous. I strive to build technology that is not only functional and secure, but guided by strong values and a sense of responsibility towards society and the ummah.

My background in Islamic education has given me a perspective that is rare in the technology industry. I believe that ethics and integrity must always precede capability. Especially in cyber security, where the line between protection and harm is thin. I carry this principle into every project, every investigation, and every line of code.

Beyond technical work, I am a reader of books on deen, psychology, and poetry. I practice CTF challenges regularly to sharpen my offensive and defensive security skills. I also speak four languages - Bahasa Malaysia, English, Arabic, and Turkish which allows me to collaborate and communicate across diverse communities and cultures.
