A Little About Me

I am Muhammad Amzar Mukmin bin Pauzan, born and raised in Banting, Selangor. My educational path is unconventional by most standards. I began my formal schooling at a tahfiz institution, progressed through two more Islamic schools, earned my SMA with Jayyid Jiddan and my STAM with Jayyid, before earning a place at UiTM to study Information Systems Management.

What makes me different is not just the academic path, but the combination of skills I carry into it: I speak four languages (Malay, English, Arabic, Turkish), I am a retired PALAPES Batch-46 cadet, a practising penetration tester, a frontend developer, and someone who reads books on deen, psychology, and poetry in his spare time.

I am not just building a career or profession in technology — I am building a version of myself that can serve the ummah, the community, and the nation with integrity.

Outside of studies, you will find me practising CTF security challenges, writing Python scripts, working on my Arabic and Turkish fluency, or simply reading. I believe a mind that does not rest on comfort is a mind that grows. And regarding my journey, every single words or speech is shows how someone like me is able to achieve and continue to pursue worldly and hereafter dreams despite coming from different backgrounds, in the end we should all reflect on succeeding on both sides
